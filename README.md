# Scientology Grand Openings, 2003 - 2026
##The buildings Scientology has chosen to formally celebrate reveal patterns of geographic concentration and variation in growth for highlighted establishments over the past 23 years. This file contains a report and data used to examine these properties to touch on the above revelations. 



## Short description of what you aimed to accomplish: 
Through my data analysis I aimed to examine the geographic concentration and growth patterns of the Church of Scientology as reflected by the establishments highlighted in their grand opening archives webpage.


## Short description of your findings: 
I found that a majority of grand openings have remained concentrated in the US, with California coming out on top for the state with the most Scientology locations. Second to the US, South Africa was the nation with the most grand openings in the past 23 years. As for growth patterns, the organization announced the most grand openings in 2012, followed by a dip in reports and a rise again in 2016. I also discovered that the organization has establishments across 6 continents, but has not announced grand openings in South America, whose countries it lists under Latin America in their future openings. 


## Summary of the data collection process, with links: I found all of the information regarding the grand openings for Scientology buildings on the organization’s grand openings archive webpage, which I proceeded to scrape using Python and Beautifulsoup in Jupyter Notebook. I also referred to other pages on Scientology’s website to gather context rather than specific pieces of data to support the written portion of my text, which are hyperlinked in the body of my article.

## Overview of the data analysis process: 
When analyzing my data I began by scraping the above Scientology webpage with Python for the locations and dates of the documented grand openings over the past 23 years. I used the information I scraped in Jupyter to analyze growth patterns and areas of geographic concentration. Through this I found its peak announcement years, areas of growth, and locations it has developed its presence the most.
